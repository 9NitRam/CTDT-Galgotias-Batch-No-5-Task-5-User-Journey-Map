## Product

**Samsung AI Family Hub Smart Fridge**

## Objective

To understand the complete user experience of the Samsung AI Family Hub Smart Fridge from awareness to long-term usage.

## User Journey Stages

### 1. Awareness

* User sees advertisements and online reviews.
* Learns about AI-powered refrigerator features.

### 2. Consideration

* Compares with other smart refrigerators.
* Checks specifications, price, and AI capabilities.

### 3. Purchase

* Purchases online or from a showroom.
* Selects preferred model and features.

### 4. Setup and Installation

* Connects the fridge to Wi-Fi.
* Installs the SmartThings app.
* Configures AI features.

### 5. Daily Usage

* Uses AI food management.
* Receives recipe suggestions.
* Monitors food inventory.
* Controls connected smart devices.

### 6. Long-Term Experience

* Gets software updates.
* Recommends the product to others.
* Continues using AI features regularly.

## Pain Points

* High purchase cost
* Dependence on internet connectivity
* Learning curve for first-time users

## Opportunities

* More affordable models
* Better offline functionality
* Enhanced AI recommendations
